Help

A green check will appear next to the count of gifts above the button when all gifts on the page are located. You may need to scroll down to load them all.

When results return, a green check (✅) signifies success, a yellow warning (⚠) means an email was found but the person has not given consent to marketing to they cannot be added to lists, and a red x (❌) means an email could not be located or the Drip operation failed.
